## Scope

- Added an enum-only `contact_duplicate_resolution_decisions` layer over already-visible contact dossier `duplicate_candidate` cues.
- Stored safe contact IDs, decision/reason enums, idempotency fingerprint/key metadata, reviewer metadata, timestamps, and fixed no-merge/no-permission-widen boundary flags only.
- Added visible-cue-filtered API routes:
  - `GET /api/contacts/:contactId/duplicate-resolution-decisions`
  - `POST /api/contacts/:contactId/duplicate-resolution-decisions`
- Kept non-duplicate contact data-quality signals on `/api/contacts/data-quality-resolutions` and rejected new duplicate-candidate writes there.
- Updated the Contacts dashboard to route duplicate cue decisions to the enum-only endpoint while keeping non-duplicate decisions on the existing route.
- Validation used synthetic data only and preserved privacy boundaries for client, matter, credential, matching-value, and reviewer-note data.

- No automatic contact merge.
- No contact field mutation.
- No hidden matter data expansion.
- No raw matched value, matched-field array, private note, or resolution note storage in duplicate decisions.
- No conflict-check mutation.
- No portal/contact permission widening.
- No new dependencies, copied source, vendored assets, provider integrations, or real client data.
