# Email Template Reviewed Outbound Preview Handoff Proof - 2026-06-30

## Scope

This proof covers the staff-only, provider-neutral handoff from an immutable published email template
version into a persisted reviewed outbound preview for one authorized matter contact.

Shipped behavior:

- New `email_template_reviewed_outbound_previews` storage and domain record for
  `reviewed_preview` posture.
- Staff `POST
/api/email/template-drafts/:templateDraftId/versions/:publishedVersionId/reviewed-outbound-previews`
  from a published version and staff `GET
/api/email/template-drafts/:templateDraftId/reviewed-outbound-previews?matterId=&limit=`.
- Server-side matter access, related-resource/matter matching, contact visibility/matter-link, email
  method, and do-not-contact checks.
- Dashboard reviewed-preview contact selector, selected published-version action, and recent preview
  list using active matter contact email methods.
- Redacted audit taxonomy/action `email_template_reviewed_outbound_preview.created`.

Preserved boundaries:

- `/api/email/previews` remains render-only and existing preview snapshots remain preview-only.
- No email sends, outbox rows, BullMQ jobs, provider calls, SMTP/IMAP settings inspection, bulk sends,
  campaigns, subscription management, automation, or permission widening were added.
- POST body does not accept `to`, `cc`, `bcc`, recipient arrays, delivery confirmation, receipts,
  provider, campaign, automation, or subscription fields.
- Persisted preview/audit metadata excludes raw recipient email addresses, provider payloads, job
  data, raw template body content, and raw audit template-body metadata.
- Tests and examples use synthetic matter/contact/template data only.
